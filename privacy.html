Privacy Policy for Email Writer

Effective Date: October 2025

Email Writer is a Chrome extension that helps users generate AI-powered email drafts directly within Gmail. To provide this functionality, the extension temporarily sends the text you select or type (such as your email body or prompt) to a secure backend server where the AI model processes and returns a response.

Data Handling

  • User input (like email content or tone selection) is transmitted securely to our API for AI generation.
  • No personal information is permanently stored or shared with third parties.
  • All processing occurs over HTTPS and data is deleted automatically after generating the response.

Your Control

You may remove the extension at any time from your browser settings. Once removed, the extension no longer has access to Gmail or any related data.
